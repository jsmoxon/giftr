How It Works

Make a list

Make a list of friends you want to give gifts to. For example, "Martha's birthday on October 5th" or "Joe's wedding on June 27". You have the option to tell us something about them or nothing at all.

Choose a gift

When the occasion is near we'll email you 3 tailored gift ideas for you to choose from. If you don't like them we'll work with you to find that perfect gift.

Relax

Once you've chosen a gift we'll take care of all the logistics and let you know when your friend received the gift.
