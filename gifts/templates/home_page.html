How It Works

Gift Ideas

Make a list of friends you want to give gifts to. For example, "Martha's birthday on October 5th" or "Joe's wedding on June 27". Tell us a bit about them to help us tailor the gift ideas.

Occasion Reminders

When the date is near we'll email you 5 or 6 tailored gift ideas for you to choose from. If you don't like them our team will work with you to find something perfect.

Easy Logistics

Once you've chosen a gift we'll take care of all the logistics and let you know when your friend received the gift.
